In this Tech, Lies and Audiotape Podcast, we examine a scenario where Steve Jobs did not die in 2011 but in fact was cyrogenically frozen and placed in suspended animation with one thing still functioning: his brain. Woken up 15 years later with time to contemplate the ultimate tech solution, Jobs has an answer for the AI resource bottleneck: human AI. One problem: If you need to go grocery shopping, you can't. While parody, this faux narrative hypothesizes about what might really happen if a major tech company such as Apple made a move to make the human the AI.
